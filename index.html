YFSN will help you to start improving your lifestyle and eating habits to boost your health with simple small changes. It's not a diet. It's not a workout. It's a lifestyle!

How it works: The key to achieving and maintaining a healthy lifestyle and weight isn't about short term dietary changes. It's about incorporating a healthy diet is easy and simple to maintain and that excludes food groups that never benefit you and will end up never missing. YFSN takes the baby steps approach to developing new habits, each new healthy habit building on the next. No more failing. No more going off. Just progress.

First

Begin by reading my book You're Fat, She's Not. Then start using this App to track your progress.

Track and Compare

The YFSN App will evaluate your daily activities and rate your progress on a daily, weekly and monthly basis. This will allow you to see exactly why you are making progress, losing weight, getting healthier, feeling more energy or why you are not making the progress you should be making. With this App you can also create a group to inspire and compare your progress with others.

Adjust

YFSN is a complete health guide for every aspect of your life. We make product suggestions, offer recipes, make daily routine recommendations and give you great stretches and exercises to help you rock our world. There are plenty of choices to suit every lifestyle and each choice leads to the better, healthier you.
